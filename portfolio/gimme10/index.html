How it works

If you're like most writers, your days are filled with work, school, family, and a million other obligations. It's hard to find enough time to work on a project that's truly your own. Gimme5 is a brainstorming tool for the writer on-the-go. Your writing workout can be stopped mid-stream and picked back up when you have a few more minutes to spare. It's perfect for those long bus rides to and from work.

Simply, create a new project and the app will generate a word for you, along with its definition and a question. Your job is to come up with five different answers to the question and build a character description using your answers for inspiration.

So ... whaddaya say?

Are you ready to gimme5?

First Set

For your first (and at this point, only) set, you will be given a word and its definition, followed by a character-based question to answer in relation to them. You need to come up with five responses before you can continue to the next round. Try to give your answers some thought. Avoid one or two word toss-off answers, you'll be thanking yourself later.

Don't worry. You got this.

Let's Build a Character

Use your thoughts below to come up with a description for a character. Go into the details of their upbringing and family, their work and personal life. What do they look like? How do they act when they think that no one's looking? You don't have to include all of your answers in creating your character. Just use them as a jumping off point!
